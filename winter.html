Rare birds in New York City

  • Summer
  • Fall
  • Winter
  • Spring

The Australian Steelback travels the furthest of all winter birds. The worst part is, it was Winter when it left it's home! Poor thing. Found in Forest Park.

Beware the Blue Bomber! It strikes from on high, shrieking all the while. You can imagine why they are becoming even rarer. Nobody likes them.

The Pink Pudge just doesn't care. If you are lucky enough to find one, don't hurry. It isn't moving before Spring. Look in Prospect Park.
